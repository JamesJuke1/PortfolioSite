Personal Projects

Marine Down

In Oct. 1983, while on a peace keeping mission to ensure the withdraw of Yasser Arafat's troops in the region. The marine corps base in Beirut was bomb by suicide bombers driving trucks filled with explosives. Many marines and memebers of NATO forces were killed in result.You take over as a young marine named Dan "Juke", a survivor of the bombings and his mission for revenge on those that killed his friends. To discover that there are plans to start WWIII. As you venture in the game you will be able to help the poeple of Lebanon and rebuild your base, which will help in your fight against Arafat.

Save The Boy

In 2100 the world has changed and only a select few can become citizens. Most of them enlist in the military to do it. You joined so your family could become citizens. No one can know about the missions that you have done, but your family is taken care of and to suprise them your return home after your term of service is up on your nephews birthday of all days. However your enemies think otherwise, the raid the party shortly after you arrive and kidnap your nephew. Your family is in shock ad looks to you to get him back, leaving you with the question, can you Save The Boy?

Alpha Omega University

Your 18 and for your summer break you decide to look at colleges. One recruiter really stood out to you so you decide to look at them first. You always thought something was different about you, you just did not expect to ever have powers. You have just joined a world filled with supernatural of all kinds. First you must pass pledge week decide what kind of supernatural being you are, are you good or will evil lure you to the darkness, are you going to be an honor student, a jock or did you come just for the attractions. The choice and grades are up to you, but choice wisely as danger lurks around every corner in a school filled with supernatural and old blood fueds.
